Problem
Psychedelic therapy is young. Best practices are still being discovered. Facilitators juggle presence, note-taking, protocol recall, and safety. Difficult experiences are not rare in naturalistic use, which raises the need for real-time de-escalation and expert backup [2]. Regulators and professional groups are pushing toward clearer documentation and safety standards, which many clinics cannot meet with manual workflows [3][4]. PubMed Psychiatric Times+1

Solution
Uncover and spread best practices faster. HarborMind learns each facilitator's style and leans into it. Every suggestion links to sources and to de-identified prior outcomes.
Preparation
Values clarification. Parts mapping. Intention priming. Set and setting checklist. Music and sensory plans. Breath ladders.
In-session
Context-aware prompts from a curated modality library. Examples: observer-self reframing, safe-container imagery, somatic pendulation, compassionate-inquiry stems, ACT reframes, coherence breathing pacing, bilateral stimulation cues, guided awe induction, double-crux conflict untangling, time-perspective shifting, inner-child repair visualization.
Integration
Narrative reconstruction, commitment contracts, tiny-habit plans, relapse-prevention cues, social-support scripts, structured meaning-making.
Presence engine
Live transcription, semantic tagging, outcome tracking. The facilitator stays eyes on the client while HarborMind builds the structured record.
Pattern seer
Recommends the next best modality by reading language and affect, then ranks options by historical effectiveness for similar contexts.
Safety net
If distress thresholds are crossed, one-click invite brings a certified remote specialist into the session with instant context. Included in the subscription under fair-use. Survey data show a meaningful tail of challenging experiences in real-world settings; rapid expert backup reduces risk and time to stabilization [2]. PubMed
Governance inside the loop
Explicit consent, encrypted storage, role-based access, audit trails. Recommendations aligned to the first comprehensive professional practice guidelines for psychedelic-assisted therapy [4]. BrainFutures
