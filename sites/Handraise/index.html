The Solution
Convert contacts into opt-in cohorts
The Mechanism
Map your network to skills, topics, and availability. Every brief includes your goal, key context, constraints, and what a good answer looks like. Responses arrive side-by-side and synthesize into one recommendation with the smallest next step.
Results credit contributors with portable proof of impact.
